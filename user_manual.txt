User manual of simplified checkers

To start the game just run the main file.
The game works as follow:
    Change your name and choose your color. Blue starts first.
    After clicking on a piece of your color the allowed moves are shown by a blue dot, click on the blue dot to make the move.
    Your piece can only go diagonally forward in the direction of your enemy. You can capture an enemy's piece by jumping diagonally over it.
    Your goal is to reach the other side of the board while keeping the enemy from raeching yours.
    When the games end the game will automatically go back to the menu.
